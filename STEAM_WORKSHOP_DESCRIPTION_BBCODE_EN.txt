[h1]Wagstaff Aurora — The Cryptic Founder[/h1]

[b]A fully self-contained character mod for Don't Starve Together.[/b]

Play as Wagstaff, the nearsighted inventor with a delicate stomach — featuring a 17-node skill tree, 4 robotic bots (MK2/MK3 tiers), automated sentry guns, dispensers, teleporters, and a dual-path affinity system (Shadow vs Celestial) with unique revive buffs.

[h1]About the Character[/h1]

Wagstaff is the "Cryptic Founder" — an inventor who found himself exactly where he wanted to be: in the Constant. Unlike the other survivors who were tricked into the realm, Wagstaff came by choice.

[b]Perks[/b]
[list]
[*]Exclusive engineering crafting (bots, sentries, dispensers, teleporters)
[*]17-node skill tree across 3 branches (Mechanical / Robotic / Allegiance)
[*]Calibrated Wrench — builds and repairs machinery, durability optimized via skill
[*]Dual affinity system — choose Shadow Engineer or Celestial Engineer (boss-locked), each with unique abilities and revive buffs
[*]Per-world progression — XP/insights saved per world, not per profile
[/list]

[b]Drawbacks[/b]
[list]
[*]Nearsighted — without goggles the world is blurred (blur shader). Needs goggles to spot hidden dangers
[*]Weak stomach — eating raw/unprepared food causes damage (configurable: -1 to -100)
[*]Unstable projection — below a configurable HP %, Wagstaff's projection starts to distort/flicker
[/list]

[b]Stats[/b]
[list]
[*]Health: 150
[*]Hunger: 225
[*]Sanity: 150
[/list]

[h1]Goggles (4 types)[/h1]

Wagstaff starts with basic goggles and can unlock superior types. Without goggles he suffers blurred vision and cannot spot hidden dangers.

[list]
[*][b]Spectoggles[/b] — Basic super goggles. Reveal hidden dangers in "peculiar" trees/rocks
[*][b]Infroggles[/b] — Heat vision. "Is everything hot or not"
[*][b]Visor[/b] — Armored lenses (protection)
[*][b]Fryfocals[/b] — Static boom focusing apparatus (shoots a static charge)
[/list]

Config option: restrict goggles to Wagstaff only.

[h1]Hamlet Items[/h1]

[list]
[*][b]Telebrella[/b] — Revolutionary walker that teleports the player to the nearest Telipad. Activation by item (right-click) or by point (click a telipad on the ground). Limited uses and configurable cooldown.
[*][b]Telipad[/b] — The Telebrella's destination. Place several to build a teleport network.
[*][b]Thumper[/b] — Revolutionary harvester. Pounds the ground to harvest resources automatically.
[/list]

Config: Telebrella uses (5-50), cooldown (0-30s), activation mode (item/point), and "misfire" (may teleport to a random telipad instead of the nearest).

Engineering constructions (Mechanical branch of the skill tree), each with MK2/MK3 tiers. All buildings are wrench-repairable and consume fuel.

[*]Synergizes with the [b]wagstaff_x2_damage[/b] skill

[/list]

[h2]Dispenser[/h2]
[list]
[*]Restores health and hunger (and sanity per affinity)
[*]Fuel capacity scales by tier (Level 1 / Level 2 / Level 3)
[*][b]Two-tier aura system (v2.0.54+)[/b], range = 4 units (DISP_RANGE):
  [list]
  [*][b]STRONG tier[/b] (affinity-active phase): 2 HP/tick = 4 HP/s for celestial at DAY, 35 sanity/min for shadow at DUSK — with visual pulse + healfx
  [*][b]WEAK tier[/b] (passive phase): 1 HP/tick = 2 HP/s celestial at DUSK/NIGHT, 20 sanity/min shadow at DAY/NIGHT — no pulse, silent
  [/list]
[*]Tick interval: 0.5s
[*][b]MK.II/MK.III[/b] — larger radius, healing improvements, "Lucky Engineer" (golden proc)
[*]It is a RESOURCE item (not combat) — the pulse is proximity-gated (builder within aura), not battle-gated

[h2]Teleporter[/h2]

[h1]Robotics: Bots[/h1]

Four robotic bots (Robotic branch of the skill tree), each with MK2/MK3 tiers. All consume fuel, have regenerable health (5 HP per 5s by default), and follow their owner.

[h2]Bot Stats Table[/h2]

Base / MK2 / MK3 health and damage values (MK3 overrides, does not stack on MK2):

[list]
[*][b]Butler Bot[/b] — 200 HP base / 200 HP MK2 / 300 HP MK3 (MK3: +100 HP). Damage: deprecated (non-combat bot).
[*][b]Buster Bot[/b] — 300 HP / 36 dmg base. MK2: 600 HP / 41 dmg (+300 HP, +5 dmg). MK3: 900 HP / 46 dmg (+600 HP, +10 dmg over base).
[*][b]Brute Bot[/b] — 1500 HP / 17 dmg base. MK2: 2100 HP / 27 dmg (+600 HP, +10 dmg). MK3: 2600 HP / 32 dmg (+1100 HP, +15 dmg over base; v2.0.53 fixed the double-bonus bug).
[*][b]Ballistic Bot[/b] — 150 HP / 16 dmg base (electric, x1.5 vs mobs). MK2: 400 HP / 28 dmg (+250 HP, +12 dmg). MK3: 500 HP / 33 dmg (+350 HP, +17 dmg over base).
[/list]

Walk speed: Buster/Butler = 12, Brute = 1.5 (walk) / 4 (run). Regen: 5 HP per 5s (Brute adds +5 per upgrade level).

[h2]Allegiance Branch (2 skills, boss-locked)[/h2]

The two affinity skills are mutually exclusive (choosing one locks the other) and each requires defeating a specific boss:

[list=1]
[*][b]Shadow's Embrace[/b] — Shadow affinity. [b]Locked by: Ancient Fuelweaver[/b]. Grants the wagstaff_shadow_possession tag. "Your inventions embrace the shadows during dusk."
[*][b]Moon's Blessing[/b] — Celestial affinity. [b]Locked by: Celestial Champion[/b]. Grants the wagstaff_celestial_possession tag. "Your inventions draw strength from the moon during the day."
[/list]

[h1]Affinity System (Shadow vs Celestial)[/h1]

The Allegiance branch lets you choose an affinity path (boss-locked, mutually exclusive). The choice affects MK3 bot behavior, MK3 building behavior, and grants a [b]unique revive buff[/b] to the player (regardless of revive source — butler haunt, meat effigy, touch stone, amulet).

[h2]Shadow Engineer (Shadow's Embrace) — locked by Ancient Fuelweaver[/h2]

For offensive players who want combat sustain. Active during DUSK.

[list]
[*]Damage dealt: [b]+50%[/b]
[*]Attack speed: [b]+25%[/b] (attack period x 0.8)
[*]Movement speed: [b]+20%[/b]
[*]Lifesteal: [b]15%[/b] of damage dealt (heals)
[*]HP on revive: Full (100%)
[*]Duration: 60s
[*]Visual: Dark tint + black/purple forcefieldfx shield
[/list]

[list]
[*]MK3 shadow bots: visual pulse active during [b]DUSK[/b] (affinity-active window)
[*]Buster MK3: summons [b]Shadow Clone[/b] (40% parent damage, 50% parent HP, 50% absorption)
[*]Brute MK3: taunts lunar-aligned enemies
[*]Shadow dispenser: 35 sanity/min at DUSK (strong tier, with pulse), 20/min at DAY/NIGHT (weak tier, silent)
[/list]

[h2]Celestial Engineer (Moon's Blessing) — locked by Celestial Champion[/h2]

For defensive players who want survivability. Active during DAY.

[list]
[*]Damage dealt: [b]+15%[/b]
[*]Max HP bonus: [b]+100[/b]
[*]Damage absorption: [b]25%[/b]
[*]HP regen: [b]5 HP/s[/b]
[*]HP on revive: Full (100%)
[*]Duration: 60s
[*]Visual: White/blue forcefieldfx shield (ruinshat-style)

[list]
[*]MK3 celestial bots: visual pulse active during [b]DAY[/b] (affinity-active window)
[*]Celestial dispenser: heals HP (2 HP/tick = 4 HP/s at DAY, strong tier with pulse; 1 HP/tick = 2 HP/s at DUSK/NIGHT, weak tier silent)
[/list]

[h2]Two-Tier Pulse System (v2.0.54+)[/h2]

MK3 bots and dispensers have an affinity visual pulse that follows gating rules:
[list]
[*][b]Bots (combat)[/b]: pulse active only when (owner near) AND (in combat) AND (affinity-active phase: DAY+celestial OR DUSK+shadow). Sentry is the exception — affinity active 24/7 (not phase-locked).
[*][b]Dispenser (resource)[/b]: pulse active only when (builder in aura radius = 4 units) AND (affinity-active phase). Not battle-gated.
[*][b]Weak/passive tier[/b]: no pulse, no healfx — just the silent reduced effect (1 HP/tick celestial off-DAY, 20 sanity/min shadow off-DUSK).
[/list]

[h1]Configuration[/h1]

The mod offers presets and granular options. Options marked with [b]*[/b] are not overwritten by presets.

[h2]Presets[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]None[/b] — does not apply a preset (uses individual options)
[*][b]Default[/b] — balanced default configuration
[*][b]Old Wagstaff[/b] — classic Wagstaff configuration
[*][b]Hamlet[/b] — Hamlet-style configuration
[/list]

[h2]Individual Options[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Language[/b] — Auto / English / Chinese — Default: Auto
[*][b]*Distortion threshold[/b] — HP % below which the projection flickers — Default: 75%
[*][b]*Poor vision blur[/b] — enable the blur shader — Default: Yes
[*][b]*Restrict goggles[/b] — only Wagstaff can wear the goggles — Default: No
[*][b]*Debug mode[/b] — debug logging — Default: No
[*][b]Peculiar object chance[/b] — chance for trees/rocks to be "peculiar" — Default: 3%
[*][b]Peculiar fail chance[/b] — chance an investigation yields nothing — Default: 40%
[*][b]Peculiar uncommon chance[/b] — chance of an uncommon reward — Default: 70%
[*][b]Weak Stomach Damage[/b] — damage from eating raw food — Default: -3
[*][b]Telebrella activation[/b] — Item (right-click) / Point (click telipad) — Default: Item
[*][b]Telebrella Misfire[/b] — may teleport to a random telipad — Default: Yes
[*][b]Telebrella Uses[/b] — uses before it breaks — Default: 15
[*][b]Telebrella Cooldown[/b] — seconds between teleports — Default: 10s
[/list]

[h1]Quick Start Guide[/h1]

[list=1]
[*]Select Wagstaff in the character menu
[*]Start with the williamgadget and Spectoggles — equip the goggles to see
[*]Collect Scrap Metal and craft a Calibrated Wrench
[*]Build a Dispenser for sustain (healing/hunger)
[*]Progress the skill tree: choose Mechanical (buildings) or Robotic (bots)
[*]In the Allegiance branch, defeat the Ancient Fuelweaver (Shadow) or Celestial Champion (Celestial) to unlock an affinity path
[*]Upgrade bots/buildings to MK2/MK3 with the Wrench (uses Scrap Metal)
[*]Use the Telebrella + Telipads for mobility
[/list]
