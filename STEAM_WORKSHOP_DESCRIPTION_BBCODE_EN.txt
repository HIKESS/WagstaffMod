[h1]Wagstaff Aurora — The Cryptic Founder[/h1]

[b]A fully self-contained character mod for Don't Starve Together.[/b]

Play as Wagstaff, the nearsighted inventor with a delicate stomach — featuring a 17-node skill tree, 4 robotic bots (MK2/MK3 tiers), automated sentry guns, dispensers, teleporters, and a dual-path affinity system (Shadow vs Celestial) with unique revive buffs.

Wagstaff is the "Cryptic Founder" — an inventor who found himself exactly where he wanted to be: in the Constant. Unlike the other survivors who were tricked into the realm, Wagstaff came by choice.

[b]Drawbacks[/b]
[list]
[*]Nearsighted — Without goggles, the world appears blurred. Goggles are required to spot hidden dangers.
[*]Weak Stomach — Eating raw or poorly prepared food causes damage (configurable: 1–100).
[*]Unstable Projection — Below a configurable Health threshold, Wagstaff's projection begins to distort and flicker.
[/list]
